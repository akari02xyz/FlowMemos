# FlowMemos Support

Welcome to the FlowMemos support page.

## What is FlowMemos?
A lightweight sticky-notes app for quick notes and color tags. Your memos are stored **only on your device**.

## FAQ
- **Data sync?** Not yet. All data stays on your device.
- **Ads?** We show a small banner via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).
- **Delete data?** Delete memos in-app or uninstall the app to remove all local data.
